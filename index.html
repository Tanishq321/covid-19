About COVID-19

What is COVID-19 (Corona-Virus)

COVID-19 is the infectious disease caused by the most recently discovered coronavirus. This new virus and disease were unknown before the outbreak begam in Wuhan, China, in December 2019.

Coronavirus are a large family of virus which may cause illness in animals or humans. In humans, several coronavirus are know to cause respiratory infections ranging from the comon cold to more severe disease such as Middle East Respiratory Syndrome (MERAS) and Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome (SARAS). The most recently dicovered coronavirus causes coronavirus disease COVID-19.
